About Me

I am a mathematics Ph.D. student at Tel Aviv University, under the supervision of Professor Leonid Polterovich and Professor Yael Karshon. My research focuses on symplectic and contact geometry. Specifically, I am interested in symplectic torus actions, methods of generating functions, and Floer homology. Before starting my Ph.D., I completed my M.Sc. degree also in Tel Aviv university under the supervision of Yael Karshon. In my free time, I volunteer as a teacher for Israel’s International Physics Olympiad team.
