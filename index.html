About Me

I am a mathematics Ph.D. student at Tel Aviv University under the supervision of Professor Leonid Polterovich and Professor Yael Karshon. My research focuses on symplectic and contact geometry. Specifically, I am interested in symplectic torus actions, methods of generating functions, and Floer homology. I am also a big fan of computer science and physics, and in my free time, I volunteer as a teacher for Israel’s International Physics Olympiad team.
